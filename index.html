What is $HISS?

A community meme token on Robinhood Chain themed after Sir Hiss — the hypnotist serpent advisor of Disney's Robin Hood (1973). No utility beyond memes, vibes, and the quiet satisfaction of being right when nobody's listening.

What is Robinhood Chain?

Robinhood's Ethereum layer two, built on the Arbitrum stack — tokenised stocks trading around the clock and plugging into DeFi as collateral. Public mainnet opened 1 July 2026 with Uniswap, 1inch and Lighter live day one.

Is there a tax?

None on buy, none on sale. The advisor watched, first-hand, what taxing the peasantry to pieces did to the last regime.

Is the liquidity safe?

Liquidity is burnt and ownership renounced. Nobody drains the coffers — not even the serpent, and he's famously slippery.
