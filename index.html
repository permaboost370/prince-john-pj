Chapter the First

The Advisor They Never Deserved

❦ ⚜ ❦

Sir Hiss is the sardonic serpent advisor of Disney's Robin Hood of the year 1973 — a hypnotist snake in a rakish red cap, given his marvellous gap-toothed lisp by Terry-Thomas. It was Hiss who hypnotised King Richard into leaving upon crusade, and so it was Hiss, not the thumb-sucking prince, whose cunning placed the crown of England upon the wrong head.

He is, quite plainly, the smartest creature at court. He reads every ambush before it springs, counsels caution before every disaster, and is proven right with tedious regularity. His reward for this is to be called an idiot, swatted with a sceptre, and stuffed head-first into a barrel of ale whenever the truth grows inconvenient.

He was always right and never once rich — a condition a great many of us will recognise. This time, the serpent keeps the upside for himself.

Chapter the Seventh

Petitions to the Serpent

❦ ⚜ ❦
What is $HISS?

A community meme token upon Robinhood Chain, themed after Sir Hiss — the hypnotist serpent advisor of Disney's Robin Hood (1973). It carries no utility beyond memes, vibes, and the quiet satisfaction of being right when nobody is listening.

What is Robinhood Chain?

Robinhood's Ethereum layer two, built upon the Arbitrum stack, carrying tokenised stocks that trade around the clock and plug into DeFi as collateral. Public mainnet opened on the first of July, 2026, with Uniswap, 1inch and Lighter live on the first day. A fitting realm for a token about being cleverer than the crown.

Is there a tax?

None upon purchase, none upon sale. The advisor watched, first-hand, what taxing the peasantry to pieces did to the last regime.

Is the liquidity safe?

Liquidity is burnt and ownership of the contract renounced. Nobody may drain the coffers — not even the serpent himself, and he is famously slippery.

The address is marked TBA until $HISS is deployed on Robinhood Chain. When it goes live it will be posted here and in the official court upon X first. Any address you see elsewhere before then is a trap — Sir Hiss would counsel against it, and for once you should listen.

It is not. $HISS is an independent, unofficial community project with no affiliation to, endorsement by, or connection with The Walt Disney Company or Robinhood Markets, Inc. All references to the character are parody and fan tribute.
