The Royal Biography

A Lion. A Tyrant. A Toddler.

Prince John is the greedy, thumb-sucking antagonist of Disney's 1973 Robin Hood — an anthropomorphic lion who seizes the crown of England while his brother King Richard is away on crusade. Voiced immortally by Peter Ustinov, he blends genuine tyranny with total emotional collapse, making him one of Disney's most entertaining villains ever animated.

He hoards gold. He over-taxes the peasants. He hypnotizes his own snake advisor. And the second things go wrong, he screams for his mother. If that isn't a memecoin, nothing is.

FAQ

What is $PJ?

$PJ is a community memecoin on Robinhood Chain themed around Prince John, the gold-hoarding, thumb-sucking lion villain of Disney's 1973 Robin Hood. It has no utility beyond memes, vibes, and an unearned sense of monarchy.

What is Robinhood Chain?

Robinhood Chain is Robinhood's Ethereum Layer-2, built on the Arbitrum stack, with 24/7 tokenized stocks that plug into DeFi. Public mainnet went live July 1, 2026, with Uniswap, 1inch, Lighter and others live on day one. Perfect chain for a token about robbing from the rich.
